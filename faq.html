Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our organic butters

What makes Deja's Butters different from other skincare brands?

Deja's Butters is a proudly Black-owned business that specializes in organic skincare for melanin-rich skin. Unlike many commercial skincare products that contain synthetic chemicals and aren't formulated with darker skin tones in mind, our butters are made with 100% natural, certified organic ingredients specifically chosen to enhance and protect melanin-rich skin. Each batch is handcrafted in small quantities by a Black woman who understands the unique needs of our community. We never compromise on purity, and we're transparent about every ingredient we use.

Are your products really organic?

Absolutely! All our base ingredients are certified organic by USDA standards. We source our shea butter from fair-trade cooperatives in West Africa, our coconut oil from sustainable farms in the Philippines, and all our essential oils from certified organic suppliers. We can provide certificates of authenticity for any ingredient upon request.

Are your products specifically for melanin-rich/darker skin?

Yes! While our products work beautifully on all skin types, they are specifically formulated with melanin-rich skin in mind. We understand the unique concerns that come with darker skin tones - hyperpigmentation, uneven skin tone, ashiness, and maintaining that natural glow. Our rich butters are packed with ingredients like shea butter, cocoa butter, and vitamin E that have been used for generations in African and Caribbean beauty traditions. They penetrate deeply to nourish melanin-rich skin, help fade dark spots naturally, and keep your skin glowing without leaving a white or ashy residue.

What does it mean that Deja's Butters is Black-owned?

Deja's Butters is founded, owned, and operated by a Black woman. Every aspect of our business - from formulation to production to packaging - is guided by our founder's vision and her personal experience with melanin-rich skin. We're proud to be part of the Black business community and are committed to economic empowerment. When you support Deja's Butters, you're directly supporting Black entrepreneurship. We also prioritize working with Black-owned suppliers and give back to our community through various initiatives. Your purchase makes a real difference!

How should I store my body butter?

Store your Deja's Butters in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Because our products contain no synthetic preservatives, they're best kept at room temperature (below 75Â°F/24Â°C). If the butter melts due to heat, simply place it in the refrigerator to resolidify - this won't affect the quality or effectiveness. With proper storage, our butters have a shelf life of 12-18 months.

Can I use these butters on my face?

Yes! All our butters are suitable for facial use, though we recommend patch-testing first if you have sensitive or acne-prone skin. Our lighter formulas like Tropical Paradise Coconut, Soothing Aloe Essence, and Nature's Aromatherapy Blend are particularly good for facial application. They're non-comedogenic and won't clog pores. For best results, apply to slightly damp skin after cleansing.

Are your products safe during pregnancy?

Our pure butters like Golden Glow Shea, Chocolate Dream Cocoa, and Green Goddess Avocado are generally safe for use during pregnancy and are actually popular for preventing stretch marks. However, if you're considering products with essential oils (like Nature's Aromatherapy Blend), we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider first, as some essential oils are not recommended during pregnancy.

Do you test on animals?

Never! We are 100% cruelty-free and always will be. We do not test our products or ingredients on animals, nor do we work with any suppliers who engage in animal testing. We believe in ethical beauty and are committed to keeping it that way. Our products are also vegan-friendly, with the exception of our Youth Revival Collagen (which contains marine collagen peptides).

How much product should I use?

A little goes a long way! Because our butters are so concentrated and pure, you only need a small amount (about a dime-sized portion) for your entire face, or a quarter-sized amount for larger areas like arms or legs. Warm the butter between your palms before applying - this helps it melt and absorb more easily. Our 4oz jars typically last 2-3 months with daily use.

What is your return policy?

We want you to love your Deja's Butters! If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return unused products within 30 days for a full refund. Due to the nature of skincare products, we cannot accept returns on opened items unless there's a quality issue. If you experience any problems with your order, please contact us at contact@dejasbutters.com and we'll make it right.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for spas, boutiques, and retailers interested in carrying Deja's Butters. We also provide bulk discounts for customers ordering multiple jars. Please email us at wholesale@dejasbutters.com for more information about our wholesale program and bulk pricing options.

How do I know which butter is right for me?

Each of our butters has unique properties:

For dry/mature skin: Golden Glow Shea, Green Goddess Avocado, Youth Revival Collagen
For sensitive skin: Soothing Aloe Essence, Tropical Paradise Coconut
For all skin types: Chocolate Dream Cocoa, Sunset Mango Bliss
For aromatherapy benefits: Nature's Aromatherapy Blend
For anti-aging: Youth Revival Collagen, Mediterranean Olive Elixir

If you're still unsure, contact us and we'll help you find the perfect match for your skin!
